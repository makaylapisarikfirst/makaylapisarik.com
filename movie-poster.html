Illustrator Movie Poster

For this school project, I had to create a movie poster. The objective was to design an 11 x 17 " poster in Illustrator about my favorite movie. The poster needed to express the knowledge and skills I acquired in my Illustrator class. The movie I choose was "As Above So Below". This movie genre is thriller, horror, and mystery. It is about a team of explorers who venture into the Paris Catacombs. There the explorers uncover a dark secret that lies beneath the city of the dead.
