Organic Honey App

For this school project I had to create an application for a fake business of my choosing. I decided to continue on with the brand Oraganic Honey from the Organic Honey Wireframe. I designed a hi-def prototype of the application within Adobe XD. I used the same styleguide as the website to keep the brand sincranized.

This is the main menu. Here the user has 4 options. The top left is the store displayed with a cart for easy recognition. The top right is the "Bee Clan". Organic Honey also wants people to have memebers to be able to access healthy honey based recipies. On bottom left corner there is a Enviromental Community button. if the user would click this option it would take them to events and newsletter where Organic Honey gets users involved with helping the community and saving the bees. Lastly on the far right bottom corner there is the blog. Here users can access Organic Honey other social media plateforms.

For Organic Honey the foundation of the company is to sell organic honey. This portion of the map users can buy organic honey, organic honey beauty products, and much more.
