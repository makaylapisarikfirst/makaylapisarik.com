First School Photoshoot

For this project, I captured Christmas themed photos for State Street Beard Co. The photos had to qualify for their social media, Etsy, and main website. I was hired and, due to circumstances, given a short window to complete the photos. I spent a day taking the raw photos for her products then took shots of individual products. I then spent a few hours editing the photos to ensure the quality was the best I could provide for State Street.
